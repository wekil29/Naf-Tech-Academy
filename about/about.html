About Naf Tech Academy

Naf Tech Academy was founded to bridge the gap between ambition and opportunity for aspiring IT professionals in our community. We’re committed to hands-on learning, mentorship, and real-world skills that empower you to launch and grow your tech career—no experience needed.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and practical IT education that opens doors for everyone—especially beginners, immigrants, and career-changers in our city.
