Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need IT experience?

No prior experience required. Our courses are designed for beginners.

Do you help with job placement?

Yes, we provide career coaching, resume help, and networking opportunities.

What if I fail the exam?

We offer additional support and guidance for retaking the exam.

Can I work while taking this course?

Yes, our flexible schedule is designed for working professionals.

What if I miss a class?

We offer makeup sessions and recordings for most classes. Just communicate with your instructor—we’re flexible.

Is this course enough to pass the CompTIA exam?

Yes — we teach directly from the official exam objectives, and our sessions are designed to prepare you for both Core 1 and Core 2 (or Security+).

Do I need to buy any equipment or software?

Just a working laptop and stable internet for online classes. We provide the rest.

How long do I have to take the certification exam after training?

Most students take the exam within 1–4 weeks after completing the course. We’ll help you decide when you’re ready.

Is the certification exam fee included?

No, the exam voucher is not included, but we provide discounts and support to help you register.

What is the class size?

We keep classes small to provide individual support and maximize your learning experience.

How do I enroll?

You can contact us directly or use the "Enroll Now" button on each course page. We’ll guide you through the next steps!

What support is available after I finish the course?

All graduates receive continued career coaching, job search help, and access to our alumni community.

Are classes online or in-person?

We offer both online and in-person options—choose what works best for you.

I have more questions. How can I reach you?

We’re here to help! Contact us or join our Telegram Channel for quick answers and updates.
